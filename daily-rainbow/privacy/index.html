Daily Rainbow

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 24, 2026

Overview

Daily Rainbow is designed as a local-first iPhone app. It helps you track a simple daily fruit and vegetable habit without requiring an account.

Data Stored by the App

Daily Rainbow stores your entries, selected daily goal, and reminder preference on your device. This information is used only to show your progress inside the app.

Data Collection

Daily Rainbow does not collect personal data, does not use third-party analytics, and does not track you across apps or websites.

Notifications

If you enable daily reminders, iOS may ask for notification permission. Reminder settings are stored on your device and can be changed from the app or from iOS Settings.

Support

For privacy questions, visit Daily Rainbow Support.
