Common Questions

Does Daily Rainbow require an account?
No. The app works without signup.

Where is my data stored?
Your entries and goal are stored locally on your device.

Do reminders work if notifications are off?
The app still works. Daily reminders only run if you choose to enable notifications in iOS.
